Selected Work

What this looks like
in practice.

Representative engagements. Details anonymised — situations, complications, and outcomes are real.

Fractional CFO
Series A SaaS · $9M Revenue · Pre-Raise

Finance function rebuilt four months before a fundraise.

Situation
Finance manager had just left. Board packs built the night before meetings. Fundraise four months away. Investors asking questions that took a week to answer.
Complication
Rebuilding the model revealed two revenue streams reported gross when they should be net — ARR overstated by ~$1.1M. Needed correction before investor conversations began.

KPI framework, rolling forecast, and board cadence built from scratch. ARR restated. Round closed on time — no surprises in diligence.

Fundraising Readiness
Pre-Series A B2B SaaS · $4M ARR

A strong product with a model that didn't tell the story.

Situation
Strong product, growing customers — but hardcoded assumptions, no cohort analysis, and a narrative that didn't match the numbers. Two investors had requested a revised model.
What we found
CAC wasn't fully allocated. Payback period extended from 11 to 17 months when corrected — still fundable, but a very different investor conversation.

3-statement model rebuilt driver-based. Series A closed 11 weeks after materials were finalised.

Financial Due Diligence
Family Office · $14M Revenue · eCommerce Acquisition

Revenue that looked clean until it was stress-tested.

Situation
Family office evaluating a platform acquisition. Financials appeared clean — revenue composition had never been broken down by channel, customer, or cohort.
What the diligence found
41% revenue in one wholesale customer on a contract expiring in 8 months. Working capital peg discrepancy adjusted the price by $380k. Neither visible in summary financials.

Full buy-side FDD delivered. Deal closed at revised terms — concentration risk renegotiated into earn-out structure.

Financial Modelling
Series B Healthtech · $22M ARR

A board model that couldn't be updated without breaking.

Situation
Board requested a new model for a strategic planning offsite. Existing model: hardcoded throughout, no scenario functionality, broke on updates.
Additional scope
Model was being used for clinical hiring forecasts — errors flowing through to headcount decisions. Scope extended to include workforce planning.

Integrated 3-statement model with scenario outputs and workforce planning. Team trained to maintain it independently.
